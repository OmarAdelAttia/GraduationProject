Best Way To Choose Your Mentor

We have 3 catergories for you to choose from, each category is designed for a specific need. That's to help you know which mentor to choose and which package you need.

Bronze Category:

If your goal is to just lead a healthy life if you need a mentor to guide you throw your day-to-day dietary decisions or your workout routines. This should be very useful if you want to keep your diet in check and keep healthy or if you have a chronic disease and want to manage it with a healthy diet.

Silver Category:

If your goal is to get in shape and you need a mentor to help you through this journey. This should be useful if you want to gain or lose weight and want some professional guidance.

Gold Category:

If you're an athlete and you have very specific dietary and fitness needs, you will find doctors and trainers from the gold category are your best choice to have your day-to-day professional guidance.
