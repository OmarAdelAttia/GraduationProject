How To Choose Your Mentor

We have 3 catergories for you to choose from, each category is designed for a specific need. Make sure to know how to choose your mentor and which package you need.

Bronze Category:

If your goal is to just lead a healthy life and you need a mentor to guide you throw your day-to-day dietary decisions or your workout routines. Bronze category should be very useful if you want to keep your diet in check and keep healthy or if you have a chronic disease and want to manage it with a healthy diet.

Silver Category:

If your goal is to get in shape and you need a mentor to help you through this journey. Silver category should be useful if you want to gain or lose weight and want some professional guidance.

Gold Category:

If you're an athlete and you have very specific dietary and fitness needs, you will find that doctors and trainers from the gold category are your best choice to have your day-to-day professional guidance.
